About uOttawa Chess Club

The uOttawa Chess Club is a community for chess enthusiasts at the University of Ottawa.

We organize regular meetings, tournaments, and social events for members of all skill levels.

Still not convinced?

Here are some of the benefits of joining:

  • Free snacks and drinks for all members at meetings
  • Multiple chess boards for all members to use
  • Free tutoring sessions for beginners

New to chess? Check out Chess.com and start playing today!
